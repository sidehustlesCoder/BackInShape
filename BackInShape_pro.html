BackInShape · Diabetes + Fitness
Rules & Guidelines
Follow every day for safe, fast results
✅ Always Do
🍳
Eat before morning gym — 2 eggs + banana minimum. Non-negotiable for diabetics.
🌾
Low GI carbs only — oats, jowar roti, brown rice, bajra, multigrain. No white rice or maida.
🥗
Include protein + fibre at every single meal — controls blood sugar spikes.
🚶
Evening walk after snack — 40–45 min brisk. Proven to lower post-meal glucose.
💧
Drink 3.5–4 litres of water daily. Start with 2 glasses on waking.
📏
Weigh every Monday morning (same time, empty stomach). Track waist monthly.
😴
Sleep 7.5 hrs minimum. Sleep debt spikes cortisol and blood sugar — kills fat loss.
🩺
Carry glucose tablets to gym. Check if dizzy mid-workout.
⚠️ Be Careful
🏃
Don't do high-intensity cardio (HIIT) until Week 5. Build base first — joint and glucose safety.
🍌
Fruit timing matters — banana only pre-workout or morning. Not at night. Prefer apple, guava, pear.
🧀
Limit full-fat paneer — use low-fat paneer or restrict to 100g/day max.
💊
If on diabetes medication, inform your doctor you're starting a new training regimen. Doses may need adjustment.
🌅
Don't skip rest days. At 41, recovery is part of the programme, not a weakness.
❌ Never Do
🚫
No fasted training — ever. Blood sugar crash risk is high, especially at 6 AM.
🍚
No white rice, maida (refined flour), white bread, or sugary rotis at any meal.
🧃
No fruit juices, packaged drinks, "diet" sodas, or sugary chai. Even "natural" juices spike glucose.
🍟
No deep-fried foods, samosas, pakoras, or restaurant fried items during the 12 weeks.
🛑
Never miss 2 consecutive days of training without a reason. Momentum is everything in Week 1–4.
